WELCOME HOME

ROOMS

Experience the blend of luxury and homely comfort at LeDream.

  • We offer six distinct room types to cater to your unique needs.
  • Each room is equipped with a host of amenities designed for your comfort and convenience.
  • Start your day right with LeDream Hotel's complimentary breakfast, included with every booking, and enjoy your welcome drinks upon arrival.

RESTO & BAR

Indulge in a unique dining experience at our rooftop restaurant, LAK's Bar + Resto.

  • Dine in style, with a stunning view that complements the exquisite flavors of our menu.
  • Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner under the stars or a lively evening with friends, LAK's Bar + Resto promises an unforgettable experience.

VIGAN

Feel the charm of Vigan City, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, right at our doorstep.

  • Immerse yourself in the rich history and vibrant culture of Vigan City, renowned for its preserved Spanish colonial architecture, cobblestone streets, and horse-drawn carriages.
  • From the captivating historic houses to the bustling markets and local delicacies, there's an adventure waiting around every corner.

SERVICES

We're committed to providing you with top-notch services to enhance your stay.

  • iWash Laundry & Dry Cleaning service, ensuring your clothing needs are taken care of.
  • Kalle Cafe' offers specialty coffee within the hotel, perfect for a morning pick-me-up or a cozy afternoon.
  • Airport pick-up and drop-off services for a hassle-free travel experience.
  • Our service van is available to take you wherever you need to go.
