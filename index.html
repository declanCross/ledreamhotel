ABOUT LE DREAM HOTEL

ABOUT OUR HOTEL

Welcome to Le Dream Hotel, your home away from home in the beautiful and historic city of Vigan. Le Dream Hotel is family-owned and operated, founded by Lorry and Engracia Salvador, who had a dream of creating a place where guests can rest and relax, while exploring the rich culture and heritage of Vigan.

One of the highlights of our hotel is our rooftop bar and restaurant, LAK’s Bar & Resto, where you can savor the best of local and international cuisine, while enjoying the stunning views of the city skyline and the sea.

Le Dream Hotel is conveniently located in Tamag, Vigan City, just a few minutes away from the University of Northern Philippines, the Baluarte Zoo, and the famous Spanish colonial architecture of Calle Crisologo. Our friendly and helpful staff can assist you with arranging tours, transportation, and other services.

At Le Dream Hotel, we are more than just a hotel. We are a dream come true. Book your stay with us today, and let us make your dream vacation a reality. We look forward to welcoming you soon.
